How We Build Your Dreams

1
Phase 01

Consultation & Layout Audit

We review your site coordinates, inspect structural foundations, understand room dimension requirements, and provide basic budget estimates.

2
Phase 02

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you manage both civil shell construction and interior work?

+

Yes, RV Construction is a full-service design-build contractor. We manage concrete framework construction, bricklaying, custom steel and gate fabrication, all the way to detailed interior styling, false ceilings, and modular carpentry.

Where in Bangalore are your primary service areas located?

+

Our head office is in Ramamurthy Nagar (Akshaya Nagar). We primary serve East and North Bangalore areas, including Kalyan Nagar, Whitefield, KR Puram, Hennur, Banaswadi, and surrounding local neighborhoods.

How do you calculate building construction and fabrication estimates?

+

Estimates are calculated per square foot based on material classes selected (Standard, Premium, Luxury). For custom steel fabrications, charges are derived from metal weight and welding hours needed. We prioritize transparent pricing with zero hidden costs.
